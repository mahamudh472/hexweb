Ukraine Risk Map

The Ukraine Risk Map is an interactive map, developed by Inherent Risks in May 2022, as an easy-to-use risk
management and journey planning tool with the primary goal of assisting organisations to identify Risk Zones in
Ukraine, and to establish where Medical, Evacuation and Crisis cover is available.

To date it has received over 25,000 unique visitors, and is widely used by global insurers, media organisations,
NGOs, humanitarians, and foreign governments as an essential travel risk management tool
